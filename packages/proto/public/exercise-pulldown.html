Lat Pulldown

Muscle group
Lats, Biceps
Equipment
Cable Machine
Category
Strength

The lat pulldown is one of the best exercises for building wide, V-tapered lats without requiring the ability to do pull-ups. Seated at a cable machine, you pull a wide bar down to your upper chest, replicating the motion of a pull-up and effectively targeting the latissimus dorsi and biceps.
