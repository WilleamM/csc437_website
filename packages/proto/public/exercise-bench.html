Barbell Bench Press

Muscle group
Chest, Shoulders, Triceps
Equipment
Barbell, Bench
Category
Strength

The barbell bench press is the gold-standard chest exercise for building upper-body pressing strength. Lying flat on a bench, you lower a loaded barbell to your chest and press it back up, engaging the pectorals, anterior deltoids, and triceps through a full range of motion.
