Conventional Deadlift

Muscle group
Hamstrings, Glutes, Back
Equipment
Barbell
Category
Strength

The conventional deadlift is one of the most demanding and rewarding compound lifts. Pulling a loaded barbell from the floor to a standing position engages nearly every muscle in the body — legs, glutes, entire back, and grip — making it the ultimate test of total-body strength.
