Romanian Deadlift

Muscle group
Hamstrings, Glutes
Equipment
Barbell
Category
Strength

The Romanian deadlift is an excellent hip-hinge movement for isolating the hamstrings and glutes. Unlike a conventional deadlift, the bar starts from a standing position and only descends as far as your hamstring flexibility allows — usually mid-shin — before returning to the top under full muscle control.
