Incline Bench Press

Muscle group
Upper Chest, Shoulders, Triceps
Equipment
Barbell, Bench
Category
Strength

The incline bench press shifts the emphasis toward the upper portion of the pectorals and the anterior deltoids by setting the bench at a 30–45 degree angle. It is a key accessory lift for building a full, rounded chest and filling out the upper chest shelf that the flat bench press underworks.
